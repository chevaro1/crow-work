Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

CAN I USE PAVILION AS MY COMPANY TRADING ADDRESS?
Yes, you can use Pavilion as your trading address for £60 per month.

HOW OFTEN CAN I USE MY MEMBERSHIP AND WHAT ARE THE OPENING HOURS?
All our memberships offer unlimited access to your home club 24/7, 365.

DO YOU OFFER A VIRTUAL SERVICE?
Yes, we do. Mail storage & sorting is £60 per month and call answering in your company name is also £60 per month.

HOW MUCH DEPOSIT IS REQUIRED:
Simply one month’s membership across the board.

HOW MANY GUESTS CAN I BRING WITH ME AS A MEMBER?
Members can invite a maximum of three accompanied guests into the club at any one time. Should you wish to bring any more than three guests we politely request that you book a meeting room.

DO I HAVE TO SIGN UP FOR A LONG CONTRACT?
Hot Desk memberships are based on a 3 or 12-month contract. All other memberships are based on a monthly rolling licence.

DO YOU OFFER CALL ANSWERING IN MY COMPANY NAME?

Yes, call answering in your company name is included in Private Office membership and above. For all our membership tiers it is £60 per month.
ARE THERE ANY HIDDEN EXTRAS?

No, you only pay extra for your telephone calls and for any meeting rooms you wish to book.
ENVIRONMENT IF I HAVE AN OFFICE CAN I CONTROL MY OWN AIR CONDITIONING?

Yes, all our offices have individual air conditioning units which are independently controlled.
DO YOU HAVE SHOWERS?

Yes, there are showers with complimentary towel service at both locations.
DO YOU HAVE LOCKERS?

Yes, for a small monthly fee we have two sizes of lockers available.

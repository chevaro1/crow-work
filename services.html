In The Heart of Crawley

Located opposite the underground station on bustling Kensington High Street, Kensington Pavilion revolutionised hot-desking and the serviced office model when it opened in 2011.

Constantly evolving, its six floors offer design-led hot-desk lounges, pods, private offices, one man offices and penthouse office suites. The building is full of bespoke artworks from Laurence Edwards bronze monkeys to the dramatic Bruce Munro chandelier in the main atrium.

The Ivy Kensington Brasserie is located at the front of the building and also serves breakfast, lunch and dinner on the large covered terrace to the rear overlooking the tranquil gardens of St Mary Abbots, with priority booking for Kensington Pavilion members.
